The Proposal

After dating Ariel for 2 months I told her that I think she would be a good wife and mother. After our Christmas trip to the Cayman Islands a year later, I was ready to ask her to marry me. The trip was incredible and relaxing but beyond that, because we were the only ones that either of us knew for thousands of miles, it felt like what it would be like to spend my life with her.

As far as Ariel knew, we were just going to Niagara Falls for a quick getaway for 2.

While the sun was rising I asked Ariel which of our vacations was her favorite. She promptly answered the Cayman Islands. I took out the engagement ring I asked Ariel to marry me. She said yes and changed her favorite vacation to Niagara Falls.
